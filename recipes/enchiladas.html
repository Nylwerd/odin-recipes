Mom's Enchiladas

Description

This recipe comes from mom's heritage. This recipe has been in her family for generations. The green chili sauce is a well kept family secret until now! Mom makes this specific recipe when she feels like the family needs a taste of home, Albuquerque New Mexico, and it does not disappoint!

Ingredients

  • 4 Skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Garlic salt to taste
  • 18 corn tortillas
  • Green chili enchilada sauce. link coming. For now use 1 28 ounce can of green chili enchilada sauce
  • 1 16 ounce package of shredded Monteray Jack cheese
  • 1 8 ounce container of sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Season chicken with garlic salt and bake for 45 mins or until juices run clear.
  3. Cool, shread, and set chicken aside.
  4. Char each of the corn tortillas over an open flame range
  5. Pour half the enchilada sauce in a medium baking dish and arrange 6 tortillas in a single layer on top.
  6. Sprinkle half the chicken, 1/3 of the cheese, 1/2 of the sour cream, and 1/3 of the remaining enchilada sauce.
  7. Repeat until none of the ingredients remain.
  8. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  9. Cool and serve
