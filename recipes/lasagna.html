Mom's Lasagna

Description

This is mom's favorite lasagna and she has been making this dish for a little over 5 years. This is a staple in the Lynn household and when she makes it we are scraping the bottom of the pan. The best part about this lasagna is the longer it cools the better it becomes! Enjoy the mom's best lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1 pound 80/20 ground beef and 1 pound of italian sausage
  • Onion and Garlic: The heart and soul of flavor one onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato Products: One can of crushed tomatos, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt Italian seasoning, fennel seed, and black pepper
  • Noodles: Store bought noodles is fine here
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • Egg: One egg will help bind the cheese together

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the Noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest
